Stay Safe Online Checklist:

1. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
2. Never share your OTP, passwords, or banking details with anyone.
3. Always verify links before clicking on them.
4. Avoid responding to suspicious emails or messages.
5. Enable two-factor authentication for extra security.
6. Do not use public Wi-Fi for online banking or payments.
7. Always check UPI payment requests before approving them.
8. Immediately report cybercrime on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
